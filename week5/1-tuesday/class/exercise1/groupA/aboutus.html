About Us


Grandma's Knittery was founded by four strangers on the internet, built with nothing but a short conversation and a few smiles. Through hard work and perseverence the site was built in a matter of hours and has become an overnight success, catering to small, old lady knitters across the globe.
Rudy is our master of Style, having studied textiles with the sherpas and wizened wise women of Nepal.Forever dreaming of llamas and the tufty clouds of the Himalayas, he can often be found perusing the markets of bangladesh in search of the perfect silk yarn. He also manages all of our branches with steadfast knowledge and a loving hand.
Ben is our director of admissions to our coven of world class knitters. Raised by parents who were the owners of a prominant nursing home chain in Southern Iowa, he got his Masters Degree in elderly lady communications from the University of Saskatchewan, while completing side coursework in underwater basket weaving.
Having travelled extensively throughout Peru and the lower hemisphere in search of the perfect pair of socks, Stacey is our head of operations. She is the heart of our design department, pulling inspiration for all our site from all over the world.
Heather is our head of security, protecting all your files and knitting patterns with her trusty banjo she stole from Jack Kerouac back in the early 1960s. Clearly the oldest of our team, she provides safety for millions of of biddies executing large scale "knitting bombs", decorating unsuspecting cities from Vietnam to Argentina.
